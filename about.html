Who We Are

GreenSteps is a non-profit organization dedicated to environmental sustainability, biodiversity conservation, and community-driven climate action. Founded in 2015, we aim to bridge the gap between local communities and global environmental goals through practical, impactful initiatives.

From tree planting and waste management to educational programs and renewable energy solutions, our work is guided by a single vision: a cleaner, greener, and more resilient planet.
